I’m a third year graduate student in the Linguistics Department at the University of Maryland, College Park where I’m also a Language Science Fellow.

Broadly, I’m interested in the semantics and pragmatics of wh-words, and their acquisition. I’m advised by Valentine Hacquard and Jeff Lidz. See projects for more.

When I’m not doing linguistics, I help take care of community cats with A Cat’s Life Rescue.
